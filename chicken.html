grilled chicken suya(Nigerian Street Style)

Tips:

Add lemon juice before grilling for tenderness

Serving:

Serve with sliced onions, tomatoes,and cucumber

Ingredients:

  • 500g chicken breast/thigh
  • 2 tablespoon suya spice
  • 2 tablespoon groundnut oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Onion slice's and tomato for garnish

Preparation

  1. Cut chicken into strips
  2. Mix with suya spice, oil, and salt
  3. Marinate for 1 hour
  4. Grill or bake for 25-30 minutes until golden brown

Advantage

  • High protein for muscle repair
  • Low fat if grilled properly
