Jollof rice (West African Classic)

Tips:

. Use basmati rice for a fragrance flavor
. Avoid adding too much water to keep the rice fluffy

Serving:

Serve hot with fried plantain, coleslow, or grilled chicken

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups long-grain parboiled rice
  • ¼ cup vegetable oil
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • 3 tomatoes (blended)
  • 2 tablespoon tomato paste
  • 2 red bell peppers (blended)
  • 1 teaspoon curry powder
  • 1 teaspoon thyme
  • 1 teaspoon salt & seasoning cubes
  • 2 cups chicken stock

Preparation:

  1. Blend tomatoes, peppers, and onions into a smooth paste
  2. Heat oil in a pot, fry chopped onions and tomato paste for 2 minutes
  3. Add the blended mix and fry until the oil separates
  4. Add washed rice and stir well
  5. Cover and cook on low heat until rice is soft and sauce is absorbed

Advantages:

  • Rich in carbohydrates for energy
  • Contains antioxidants from tomatoes and peppers
