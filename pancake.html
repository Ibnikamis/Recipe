Pancakes (Breakfast Special)

Tips:

. Add mashed banana or cinnamon for extra plavor

Serving:

Serve with honey, syrup, or fresh fruits

Ingredients:

  • 1 Cup flour
  • 1 Egg
  • 1 Cup milk or water
  • 1 Tablespoon sugar
  • 1 Tablespoon oil or melted butter
  • A pinch of salt

Preparation:

  1. Mix all ingredients until smooth
  2. Heat a non-stick pan, add a little oil
  3. Pour batter and cook both sides until golden

Advantages:

  • Privides quick energy
  • Easy to digest and customize(Sweet or Savory)
