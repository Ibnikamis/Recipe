Spaghetti Stir-Fry

Tips:

. Don't overcook spaghetti
. Add a sprinkle of sesame oil for Asian flavor

Serving:

Serve hot with chilled fruit juice

Ingredients:

  • 200g Spaghetti
  • 1 Onion
  • 1 Green pepper
  • 1 Carrot(Sliced)
  • 1 Tablespoon soy sauce
  • 2 Tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 Teaspoon seasoning cubes
  • Optional: Chicken or shrimp

Preparation:

  1. Boil spaghetti and drain
  2. Fry onions, peppers, and carrot in oil
  3. Add soy sauce and seasoning
  4. Add spaghetti and mix well

Advantages:

  • Balanced carb and vegetable meal
  • Easy and quick to prepare
