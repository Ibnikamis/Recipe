Fried Plantain & Egg (Dodo And Egg Sauce)

Tips:

. Use semi-ripe plantain for less sweetness

Serving:

Serve hot as a breakfast or light dinner

Ingredients:

  • 2 Ripe plantains
  • 2 Eggs
  • 1 Onion, chopped
  • 1 Tomato
  • 1 Tablespoon oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Preparation:

  1. Peel and slice plantains, then fry until golden
  2. In another pan, fry onnions and tomatoes
  3. Add beaten eggs, salt, and pepper
  4. Stir until scrambled and well mixed

Advantages:

  • Great combination of potassium (From plantain)
  • And protein (From eggs)
