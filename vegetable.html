Vegetable Soup (Efo Riro Style)

Tips:

. Don't overcook the vegetables to keep nutrients

Serving:

Serve with rice, yam, or swallow (Like pounded yam or eba)

Ingredients:

  • 2 Cups spinach or fluted pumpkin leaves
  • 2 Medium tomatoes(blended)
  • 1 Red bell pepper
  • 1 Onion
  • Palm oil(2 tablespoon)
  • Smoked fish or beef
  • 1 Teaspoon crayfish powder
  • Salt and seasoning cubes

Preparation:

  1. Heat palm oil, add onion and blended tomato/pepper mix
  2. Fry till thick
  3. Add meat/fish, seasoning, and crayfish
  4. Add vegetables and stir well for 5 minutes

Advantages:

  • Rich in vitamins and minerals
  • Good source of iron and fiber
