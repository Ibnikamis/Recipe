Fruit salad(Refreshing Dessert)

Tips:

. Chill before serving for better taste
. Add lemon juice to prevent browing

Serving:

Serve cold in a transparent glass bowl for a colorful look

Ingredients:

  • 1 Apple
  • 1 Banana
  • 1 Pineapple Slice
  • ½ Cup of watermelon
  • ½ Cup yogurt or honey

Preparation:

  1. Wash and dice all fruits
  2. Mix them in a bowl
  3. Add yogurt or honey and stir gently

Advantages:

  • Boosts immunity with vitamins
  • Hydrating and low in calories
